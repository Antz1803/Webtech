Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

This program develops essential IT and software development skills, preparing students for roles such as:

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)
  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS)
  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCPE)

Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN)

This program provides comprehensive training for a successful nursing career with a path to become:

  • Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN)

Bachelor of Science in Business Management (BSBM)

This program offers a solid foundation in business, management, and entrepreneurship, preparing students for diverse career paths. Students can specialize in:

  • Bachelor of Science in Business Administration Major in Financial Management (BSBA-FM)
  • Bachelor of Science in Business Administration Major in Marketing Management (BSBA-MM)
  • Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management (BSTM)
  • Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management (BSHM)
×

Bachelor of Science in Education (BSED)

This program prepares students to become inspiring educators and mentors, equipping them for impactful roles in the education field. Possible career paths include:

  • Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEED)
  • Bachelor of Secondary Education Major in English (BSED-ENGLISH)
  • Bachelor of Secondary Education Major in MATH (BSED-MATH)

Game Rules

3. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (vertically, horizontally, or diagonally) wins.

4. If all 9 squares are filled and no player has 3 in a row, the game is a tie.
